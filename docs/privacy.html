Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 21, 2024

Overview

Inkling is a local-first note-taking and calendar application. Your privacy is our priority - all your data stays on your device.

Data Storage

All of the following data is stored locally on your device only:

  • Notes and documents
  • Calendar events
  • Folders and organization
  • Application settings
  • Conversation history with AI features

We do not operate any servers that store your personal data.

Google Calendar Integration

When you optionally connect Google Calendar:

  • What we access: Read-only access to your Google Calendar events and your email (to display connection status)
  • How we use it: Calendar events are synced to your local database only
  • What we don't do: We do not store your Google data on external servers, share it with third parties, or modify your Google Calendar

Google OAuth tokens are stored locally on your device. You can disconnect your Google account at any time from Settings.

AI Features

If you use AI-powered features (chat, summarization, etc.):

  • AI requests are sent directly to your configured AI provider (OpenAI, Anthropic, Ollama, etc.)
  • We do not proxy or store these requests
  • Your API keys are stored locally on your device

Analytics & Tracking

Inkling does not include any analytics, tracking, cookies, or tracking pixels.

Data Sharing

We do not share any of your data with third parties because we do not have access to your data.

Contact

For privacy questions, please open an issue at: GitHub Issues
